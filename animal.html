My Favourite Animal : DOG


The dog or domestic dog is a domesticated descendant of the wolf. The term “domestic dog” refers to any of several hundred breeds of dog in the world today.

Domestic dogs are kept as pets,there are many breeds which are capable of surviving on their own, whether it’s in a forest or on city streets.




Benefits of having Dog

  • Dogs are Friendly that makes us feel not to feel alone.
  • Dogs are bestfriends for babies and childrens.
  • Dogs help you us to distract from stress.
  • Dogs help us cope with our issues.
  • Dogs encourage you to move.
  • Dogs make you more attractive-even virtually.
  • Dogs make us more social.
